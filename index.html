Top 5 books of all Time

Here are 5 of the most influential and widely celebrated books of all time

  1. To Kill a Mockingbird

    2. Genre: Fiction / Social Justice
    Why it's great:
    A deeply moving story about racial injustice, morality, and childhood in the American South.
    Its themes of empathy and moral courage remain relevant today.

  2. 1984

    3. Genre: Dystopian Fiction / Political
    Why it's great:
    A chilling look at totalitarianism and surveillance.
    Phrases like Big Brother and thoughtcrime entered the real-world vocabulary.

  3. The Great Gatsby

    4. Genre: Classic Romance / Social Commentary
    Why it's great
    Sharp wit, timeless romance, and brilliant commentary on class and gender roles in 19th-century England.

  4. Pride and Prejudice

    5. Genre: Classic Romance / Social Commentary
    Why it's great:
    Sharp wit, timeless romance, and brilliant commentary on class

  5. The Catcher in the Rye

    6. Genre: Coming-of-Age / Fiction
    Why it's great:
    A poignant exploration of teenage angst, alienation, and the search for identity.
    Its influence on modern literature and culture is profound.
